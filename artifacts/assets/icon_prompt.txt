APP ICON PROMPT — 好孕日記 HaoYun Diary (1024×1024, iOS App Store)
=================================================================

Subject / Concept
-----------------
A minimalist, emotionally-warm app icon for a Taiwan-focused IVF companion app.
Central motif: a softly-abstracted "diary leaf" silhouette — a rounded calendar
page with a single small sprouting seedling (two paired leaves + curved stem)
rising out of its top edge. The seedling symbolizes new life / 好孕 ("good
pregnancy"). No faces, no babies, no medical syringes — the imagery must read
as caring and reassuring, not clinical or intimidating.

Composition & Framing
---------------------
- Square 1024×1024 px canvas, sRGB, 72 dpi.
- Follow Apple's iOS icon grid: keep all meaningful subject matter inside the
  ~816×816 safe-area square (≈20% margin from each edge). Corners remain pure
  (Apple applies the rounded mask automatically — do NOT pre-round corners).
- Subject occupies roughly 55–65% of the frame, optically centered, with a
  very slight vertical bias upward so the seedling sits on the optical center.
- No drop shadow outside the icon silhouette; internal soft shadow allowed.
- Absolutely no gloss, no bevel, no skeuomorphic paper edges — flat-modern.

Color Palette
-------------
Primary background: a soft warm gradient from #FDE8EC (top-left, blush pink)
  to #FFF5E6 (bottom-right, cream). The gradient must be extremely gentle —
  reads as nearly solid on a 60×60 px Home Screen render.
Primary subject (diary page): #FFFFFF with a 2 px inner outline in #F4C8D1.
Accent 1 (seedling leaves): #7FB77E (calm sage green) with a single highlight
  leaf at #A8D5A3.
Accent 2 (stem + tiny heart dot on the diary page): #E57EA3 (muted rose).
Optional micro-detail: one faint horizontal ruled line on the diary page at
  10% opacity #C9A0A8. No text, no numerals, no logotype.

Style
-----
Flat vector, geometric, soft-rounded corners on every inner shape
(≥6 px radius). 2D only — no 3D rendering, no photoreal textures. Think of
a cross between modern Taiwanese maternity brand aesthetics (茂盛 / 送子鳥 /
NUWA) and Apple Health / Flo Period iconography. Gentle, clean, trustworthy.

Hard Constraints (no exceptions)
--------------------------------
1. NO TEXT of any kind in the icon — no "好孕", no "HY", no letters, no
   numerals. Apple App Store guidelines + Human Interface Guidelines favor
   textless icons.
2. NO human figures, faces, babies, or body parts.
3. NO medical instruments (syringes, vials, stethoscopes) — the app must not
   read as a medical device (Apple review risk per guideline 1.4.1).
4. NO trademarked brand marks (no LINE green, no Apple logo, no Flo tulip).
5. NO transparency in the background — must be a fully opaque 1024×1024
   PNG. Background gradient must cover all four corners.
6. NO photography or photographic textures; pure vector/flat illustration.
7. Readable at 60×60 px Home Screen size: test by mentally scaling — the
   seedling must remain distinguishable from the diary page silhouette.

Deliverable
-----------
Single PNG, 1024×1024, sRGB, no alpha. Named icon.png.
